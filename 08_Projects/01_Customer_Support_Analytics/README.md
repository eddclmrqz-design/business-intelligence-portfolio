# Customer Support Operations Analytics Platform

## Project Overview

The Customer Support Operations Analytics Platform is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution designed for **SupportSphere Global**, a fictional multinational customer support organization.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how modern Business Intelligence teams design data models, build reporting pipelines, develop executive dashboards, and generate actionable insights from operational data.

Rather than using confidential production data, this project utilizes a realistic synthetic dataset that simulates enterprise customer support operations while preserving privacy.

---

## Business Problem

SupportSphere Global has experienced continuous growth in customer support demand across multiple regions and communication channels.

As ticket volume increased, leadership observed:

- Increasing Initial Response Time (IRT)
- Declining SLA compliance
- Uneven workload distribution between queues
- Growing backlog of unresolved cases
- Inconsistent Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)

Existing reporting was fragmented across multiple spreadsheets and operational reports, making it difficult for leadership to identify performance bottlenecks and make timely operational decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

The platform aims to:

- Centralize operational reporting
- Measure support performance using standardized KPIs
- Monitor SLA compliance
- Analyze customer support case journeys
- Evaluate agent productivity
- Identify operational bottlenecks
- Deliver executive-ready dashboards
- Support data-driven decision making
