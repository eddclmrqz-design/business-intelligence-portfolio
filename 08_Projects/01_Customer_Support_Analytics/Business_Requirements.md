# Business Requirements Document

## Project

Customer Support Operations Analytics Platform

---

# 1. Business Background

SupportSphere Global is a multinational customer support organization providing customer service solutions for enterprise technology clients across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America.

The organization manages millions of customer interactions annually across multiple communication channels including Chat, Email, and Messaging.

As customer demand continues to grow, executive leadership requires a centralized Business Intelligence platform capable of monitoring operational performance, measuring service quality, and identifying opportunities for continuous improvement.

---

# 2. Business Problem

Operational reporting is currently fragmented across multiple spreadsheets and disconnected reports.

This makes it difficult for business leaders to answer critical operational questions such as:

- Which queues are consistently missing SLA?
- Which regions require additional staffing?
- What factors contribute to increasing Initial Response Time (IRT)?
- How does Response Received (RR) performance vary across queues?
- Which operational teams require management attention?
- How is Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) trending over time?

Without centralized reporting, operational decisions are reactive instead of proactive.

---

# 3. Business Objectives

The project aims to deliver a centralized analytics platform that enables leadership to:

- Monitor support operations in near real-time
- Measure operational KPIs consistently across all business units
- Identify SLA risks before breaches occur
- Improve workforce planning and resource allocation
- Increase customer satisfaction
- Reduce operational bottlenecks
- Support executive decision-making using trusted data

# 5. Scope

The platform will include analytics for:

- Case Volume
- Initial Response Time (IRT)
- Response Received (RR)
- SLA Compliance
- Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
- Queue Performance
- Agent Productivity
- Resolution Time
- Backlog
- Aging Cases

---

# 6. Success Criteria

The solution will be considered successful if it enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor operational performance from a centralized dashboard
- Reduce manual reporting effort
- Identify operational risks earlier
- Standardize KPI calculations
- Improve visibility across all support queues

---

# 7. Assumptions

This portfolio project uses a synthetic dataset generated specifically for demonstration purposes.

The data is designed to simulate realistic enterprise customer support operations while ensuring no confidential or proprietary information is exposed.

Business processes, KPIs, and reporting logic are inspired by real-world customer support environments but do not represent any specific organization or client.
